Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I know the vegetables are fresh?

A: When you place an order, the farmer receives a notification immediately. The crops are harvested within hours and shipped directly.

Q: How does AI price prediction help me?

A: It prevents middleman exploitation. The system forecasts the value using historic data, crop seasons, and demand/supply to ensure fair prices for both sides.

Q: How does regional shipping work?

A: Sourcing is optimized based on geographic distance. We match you with regional farmers to reduce freight time and carbon emissions.
